========================================
ESCAPE THE DUNGEON
Python Console Game
Version 1 Design Document
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Escape the Dungeon is a turn-based dungeon crawler made entirely in Python and played through the console.

The player progresses through ten floors of a dungeon, choosing paths and making decisions to survive and become stronger. The goal is to defeat the Cave King on Floor 10 and escape.

The game is based around risk versus reward.

Fight stronger enemies to become more powerful, or rest and recover to stay alive.

There are no graphics in V1. Everything is text-based.


========================================
GOAL
========================================

Reach Floor 10 and defeat the Cave King.

Win Condition:
- Defeat the Cave King.

Lose Condition:
- HP reaches 0.

========================================
BATTLE SYSTEM
========================================

Battles are turn-based.

Player turn first.

Then enemy turn.

Player actions:

1. Attack

2. Special

3. Item


========================================
ATTACK
========================================

Damage Formula:

Damage =
STR
+ random weapon damage bonus
- enemy DEF

Minimum damage should always be at least 1.

Example:

Player STR = 5

Weapon roll = +3

Enemy DEF = 2

Damage = 6


========================================
SPECIAL
========================================

50% chance:

Damage =
STR × 3

Weapon bonus is NOT added.

50% chance:

Missed!

Deals no damage.


========================================
ITEMS DURING BATTLE
========================================

Items can be used during battle.

Using an item consumes one turn.

========================================
CORE PHILOSOPHY
========================================

Escape the Dungeon is designed around meaningful choices.

Fight stronger enemies and become powerful, but risk dying.

Rest and recover, but sacrifice upgrades.

Spend gold now, or save for stronger weapons.

Use powerful items, or save them for the boss.

The player's choices determine whether they are strong enough to survive the Cave King and escape the dungeon.
